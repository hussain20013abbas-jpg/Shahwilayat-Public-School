## Full Description (Max 4000 characters)
**Shahwilayat Public School: Your All-in-One School Management Companion**

Welcome to the official mobile application for Shahwilayat Public School. Designed to streamline communication and management, our app provides a seamless experience for students, parents, and administrators.

### 🎓 Key Features for Students & Parents:
- **Academic Dashboard:** Access your homework, classwork, and syllabus anytime, anywhere.
- **Real-time Attendance:** Stay updated with daily attendance records and monthly reports.
- **Digital Wallet & Canteen:** Pay for canteen meals securely using your student ID. Track your spending and balance in real-time.
- **Exam Schedules:** View datesheets and exam timings directly on your phone.
- **Online Classes:** Join live sessions with integrated meeting links for a smooth learning experience.
- **Announcements:** Never miss an update! Get instant notifications for school news and events.

### 🛠️ Key Features for Administrators:
- **Student Management:** Effortlessly manage student profiles, grades, and records.
- **Financial Control:** Oversee canteen transactions, manage student wallets, and distribute funds.
- **Academic Planning:** Update syllabus, schedules, and datesheets with a few clicks.
- **Attendance Tracking:** Mark and monitor attendance for the entire school with ease.

### 🔒 Secure & Reliable
- **Privacy First:** Your data is protected with industry-standard encryption.
- **Role-Based Access:** Personalized experience for students, admins, and canteen staff.
- **User-Friendly Interface:** Clean, modern design optimized for mobile devices.

**About Shahwilayat Public School**
Shahwilayat Public School is committed to providing quality education and fostering a digital-first environment for its community. This app is part of our mission to integrate technology into everyday school life for better efficiency and transparency.
