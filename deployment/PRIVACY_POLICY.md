# Privacy Policy for Shahwilayat Public School App

**Effective Date:** March 20, 2026

Shahwilayat Public School ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Shahwilayat Public School mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our service to you.

### Types of Data Collected:
*   **Personal Data:** While using our App, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to:
    *   Full Name
    *   Roll Number
    *   Grade, Class, and Section
    *   Parent/Guardian Contact Information (Email, Phone)
    *   CNIC (for authentication purposes)
    *   Student Photo (for profile identification)
*   **Usage Data:** We may also collect information on how the App is accessed and used ("Usage Data"). This Usage Data may include information such as your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g., IP address), browser type, browser version, the pages of our App that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, unique device identifiers, and other diagnostic data.

## 2. Use of Data
Shahwilayat Public School uses the collected data for various purposes:
*   To provide and maintain the App
*   To notify you about changes to our App
*   To allow you to participate in interactive features of our App when you choose to do so
*   To provide student and parent support
*   To monitor the usage of the App
*   To detect, prevent, and address technical issues
*   To manage student canteen wallets and transaction history

## 3. Transfer of Data
Your information, including Personal Data, may be transferred to — and maintained on — computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ from those from your jurisdiction.

## 4. Disclosure of Data
### Legal Requirements
Shahwilayat Public School may disclose your Personal Data in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to:
*   To comply with a legal obligation
*   To protect and defend the rights or property of Shahwilayat Public School
*   To prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with the App
*   To protect the personal safety of users of the App or the public
*   To protect against legal liability

## 5. Security of Data
The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Our App is intended for use by students and their parents/guardians. We collect information about students only with the consent of their parents or guardians as part of the school enrollment process.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
