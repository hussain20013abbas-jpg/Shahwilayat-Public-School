# App Icon & Screenshot Suggestions

## 1. App Icon Design
Your app icon is the first thing users see. It should be clean, professional, and recognizable.

### Icon Design Suggestions:
*   **Primary Color:** Indigo (#4F46E5) or Navy Blue (#1E1B4B).
*   **Symbol:** A stylized graduation cap, a book, or the school's official crest.
*   **Style:** Flat design with subtle shadows. Avoid using text in the icon.
*   **Size:** 512x512 pixels (PNG format).

### Free Design Tools:
*   **Canva:** Use their "Logo" or "App Icon" templates.
*   **Figma:** Great for creating vector-based icons.
*   **Android Asset Studio:** Use this to generate adaptive icons for all Android versions.

## 2. Screenshot Suggestions (Play Store)
You need at least 2-8 screenshots for your Play Store listing.

### Must-Have Screenshots:
1.  **Welcome/Login Page:** Show the clean login interface with the school logo.
2.  **Student Dashboard:** Highlight the "Wallet Balance" and "Recent Activity" cards.
3.  **Canteen System:** Show the transaction history or the "Pay for Meal" interface.
4.  **Academic Records:** Display a sample of homework or syllabus entries.
5.  **Admin Panel:** Show the student management table or financial overview.
6.  **Online Classes:** Highlight the "Join Meeting" button and class schedule.

### Screenshot Tips:
*   **Add Captions:** Use a short, bold caption at the top of each screenshot (e.g., "Manage Your Wallet", "Stay Updated with Homework").
*   **Device Frames:** Place your app screenshots inside a modern Android phone frame (e.g., Pixel 8).
*   **Resolution:** 1080x1920 pixels (Portrait) or 1920x1080 pixels (Landscape).
